Raft Building
- Integrated into a game focused on raft racing
- The rafts can be customised by using different materials for them and by adding different attachments that modify the stats
- Stats:
	- floatability
	- speed
	- durability
	- (BONUS) style
- The user can compete against friends or pre-made players
- The first raft to cross the finish line wins the main prize
- There are other prizes to be won
